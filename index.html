Simple Persistent Scheduler

For those of us with consistent routines but inconsistent start/end times.

This simple scheduling tool allows you to create a schedule that will remain accessible from the same device. Everything is saved to your device, it never gets sent anywhere else or shared with anyone.

Intentionally simple and limited - I made this to help manage my inconsistent sleep patterns. No logging in or accounts needed, but no fancy features either.

Add new tasks and their time in minutes. Choose your intended start or end time. Checkboxes don't do anything, they're just for you.

Click "Calculate" to calculate your schedule and save it for next time. Previous schedules will be overwritten. Click "Clear Schedule" to reset everything, including your saved schedule.
