Simple Persistent Scheduler

When you have consistent routines at inconsistent times

This simple scheduling tool allows you to create an editable saved schedule.

No logging in, creating accounts, or downloading apps...but no fancy features either.

Only one schedule available for now. (Want more? Consider paying me.)

Add new tasks and their time in minutes.

Click "Add Task" to add a new task, or "X" to remove a task.

Choose your start or end time. Start and end times are not saved and can be removed with the "Clear" button.

Click "Calculate!" to calculate your schedule and save it for next time. Any previous schedules will be overwritten.

Click "Clear Everything" to reset everything, including your saved schedule.

Everything is saved to your device. No information gets sent anywhere else or shared with anyone else.

Want to double check? The code is shared on Github. Feel free to contribute!
